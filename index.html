About Me

I’m a product manager with a passion for building tools that help teams work smarter — especially at the intersection of AI, compliance, and enterprise workflows. I believe the best products are born from a deep understanding of the people who use them.

My background spans product strategy, user research, and cross-functional leadership. I’ve worked closely with engineering, design, and stakeholders to take products from zero to one and from one to scale.

Recently, I’ve been focused on the AI space — exploring how emerging capabilities can be shaped into products that are not just powerful, but trustworthy and governable. I’m currently honing my prototyping skills through an AI Prototyping for PMs course.

Case Study 06 In the Lab PM Bootcamp

Rentwise Austin

Problem: First-time renters in Austin, TX have no clear way to understand what they can realistically afford — leading to rent burden, financial stress, and poor housing decisions.

Solution: Rentwise is an AI-powered affordability calculator that takes income, expenses, and savings goals and returns Conservative, Moderate, and Stretch rent budgets with Austin-specific market context, financial literacy resources, and personalized recommendations. Delivered as a full PM package: user personas, user stories, epics, PRD, one-pager, GTM strategy, 3-year roadmap, and working prototype.
